What is a World Tourist?

Tourists across the globe.

parkrun runs free, weekly 5km events in more than 20 countries across five continents. This club is for the ones who've chased that stamp collection furthest.

The definition

This group is for like-minded parkrun tourists who have completed at least one parkrun in each of FOUR (4) parkrun countries, as listed on parkrun.com/countries.

England, Wales, Scotland and Northern Ireland all count as part of the UK for this definition, so they aren't treated as four separate countries on their own.

How it works

A quick tour of the dashboard

The dashboard is split across three tabs, each built to be explored rather than just read. Click around and the data responds.

Global Overview
01

Global Overview

A live world map plotting every event visited this week, alongside total members, weekly participants and the volunteer-to- parkrunner split.

Try it: hover over any bubble on the map to see which event it represents and how many World Tourists turned up.
Member Explorer
02

Member Explorer

A full breakdown of every parkrunner's result for the week: country, event, time and finishing position, plus a ranked list of the most visited events.

Try it: click a country flag to filter the whole table down to just that country's results.
Weekly Trends
03

Weekly Trends

Tracks the club's growth over time: registered members, weekly participation, countries visited and engagement rate, all on a rolling date range.

Try it: adjust the date range selectors to zoom in on a specific stretch of weeks and watch every chart update together.
Live dashboard

Explore it for yourself

This is the live, interactive report. Hover the map, click a flag, drag the date range. It updates weekly with the latest World Tourist activity.

Tip: For the best experience on mobile, rotate your phone to landscape before exploring the report.
