Quick tutorial

Click, filter and explore.

The report is fully interactive. Here are five quick ways to get the most out of it.

01

Select a category

Click an age group, club, milestone or chart item to filter the rest of the report.

02

Choose a table row

Select a participant or result to highlight the related information across the page.

03

Hover for detail

Move your cursor over charts and data points to reveal additional information.

04

Use the KPI cards

Select the KPI cards across the top row of the report to move between the different report pages.

05

Reset your selections

Use the reset button in the bottom-right corner of the report to clear selections and start again.
