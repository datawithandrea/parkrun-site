Quick tutorial

Click, filter and explore.

The dashboard is fully interactive. Here are five quick ways to get the most out of it.

01

Start with a chart

Click an age group, club, milestone or chart to instantly filter the page and uncover different stories.

02

Navigate the dashboard

Use the KPI cards across the top of the page to move between the different dashboard pages.

03

Hover for detail

Hover over charts and data points to reveal additional information and exact values.

04

Go back to the overview

Click the parkrun logo in the top-left corner of the page to return to the dashboard home page.

05

Reset your selections

Use the reset button in the bottom-right corner of the page to clear all filters and start exploring again.
