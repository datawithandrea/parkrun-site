Quick tutorial

Click, filter and explore.

The report is fully interactive. Here are five quick ways to explore the Jeune Park event data.

01

Start with a chart

Click an age group, club, milestone or chart to instantly filter the report and uncover different stories.

02

Navigate the report

Use the KPI cards across the top of the report to move between the different report pages.

03

Hover for detail

Hover over charts and data points to reveal additional information and exact values.

04

Go back to the overview

Click the parkrun logo in the top-left corner of the report to return to the dashboard home page.

05

Reset your selections

Use the reset button in the bottom-right corner of the report to clear all filters and start exploring again.
